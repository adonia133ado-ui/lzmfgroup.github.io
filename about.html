About MetalFusion

Building bridges between Asian manufacturing and global industry since 2010.

Our Story

Founded in Liuzhou, Guangxi — a historic industrial hub in southern China — Liuzhou MetalFusion Group started as a specialized supplier of industrial automation components. Over the years, we have expanded our portfolio to serve a broader range of industries including fluid control, construction materials, and general industrial procurement.

Our name "MetalFusion" reflects our core competency: fusing metal products with engineering solutions. We don't just supply parts — we help our clients find the right specifications, ensure quality compliance, and manage end-to-end logistics from factory floor to project site.

Today we serve customers in 12+ countries across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and South America, with a growing presence in European and North American markets.
